This clause details the terms for ESPN magazine subscriptions, including automatic renewal. Your subscription will automatically renew at the prevailing renewal rate unless you proactively cancel. It specifies that the renewal rate may be higher than the introductory rate.

  • Cancellation Process: To cancel, users must call ESPN Customer Care. There is no online cancellation option mentioned.

Consumer Impact: The requirement to call for cancellation is less convenient than online options and can lead to unwanted renewals. The variable 'prevailing renewal rate' introduces uncertainty regarding future costs.
