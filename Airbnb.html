The indemnification clause requires you to defend, indemnify, and hold Airbnb harmless from any claims, liabilities, damages, losses, and expenses (including legal and accounting fees) arising out of your breach of these Terms, your improper use of the Airbnb Platform, or your interaction with any Member. Airbnb explicitly states that it does not own, control, offer, or manage any Listings or Host Services. If you encounter issues with a booking (e.g., inaccurate listing, unsafe accommodation), your primary recourse is against the Host, not Airbnb, making it harder to seek resolution from the platform itself.
